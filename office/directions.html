DIRECTIONS


From Northside Jacksonville:

    From North of the airport:

    Take I-95 South to Exit 362A (I-295 South, Eastbound).

    Use Exit 53 to turn onto J.Turner Butler Blvd (FL-202 E) going East (towards the beaches).


    -- or --

    From South of the airport:

    Take I-95 South to Exit 344 toward FL-202.

    Head East onto J.Turner Butler Blvd (FL-202 East) towards the beaches.


    -- then --

    Get off J.Turner Butler Blvd (FL-202 E) at Hodges Blvd and go Left.

    Take a Right at the traffic light onto Sutton Park Drive. Walgreen’s Drugs is on that corner.

    Go past Burger King and Landstar then Right at the next street – Sutton Park Court.

    Take your first Left. It’s the second building on the right (#1504).

    Use the intercom in the waiting room to announce your arrival.


From Orange Park & Southside:

    From Orange Park, take the Buckman Bridge (I-295 E) to Exit 53A onto J. Turner Butler Blvd (FL-202 East).


    -- or --

    From South of Jacksonville, take I-95 North to Exit 337 (I-295 North).

    Take I-295 N to Exit 53A onto J. Turner Butler Blvd (FL-202 East).


    -- then --

    Get off Butler Blvd. at Hodges Blvd. and turn Left.

    Take a Right at the traffic light onto Sutton Park Drive. Walgreen’s Drugs is on that corner.

    Go past Burger King and Landstar then Right at the next street – Sutton Park Court.

    Take your first Left. It’s the second building on the right (#1504).

    Use the intercom in the waiting room to announce your arrival.


From the Beaches:

    You can take either JTB, Beach Blvd. or Atlantic Blvd. to Hodges Blvd.

    On Hodges, turn East at the traffic light next to the Walgreen’s Drug (at the corner of the Publix Shopping Center) onto Sutton Park Drive.

    Go past Burger King and Landstar then Right at the next street – Sutton Park Court.

    Take your first Left. It’s the second building on the right (#1504).

    Use the intercom in the waiting room to announce your arrival.
